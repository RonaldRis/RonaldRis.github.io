Who are we?

We are a 100% Salvadoran entrepreneurial company in the beverage industry that makes alcohol handcrafted with fruits grown in our country.





Mission

To provide an exceptional taste and high quality of beverages to every person with a desire for fun.





Vision

To be the best handcrafted producer in the alcoholic beverage industry in El Salvador.





Our History

Our business idea started as a homework that made us wondering “How can we get in successfully
to the business world?” Many ideas came at once that moment. Truth be told, we decided too fast.
We had decided to open a craft store, but sadly none of us possessed enough skills for this work.
One day Ever suddenly got an idea we hadn’t thought yet. He got the initial idea of putting up a bar,
which was sustained and supported modified by all of us. We wanted to create a new bar style which doesn’t
exist in our city until now. We decide that our products would be made from fruits. Due to the absence of
these businesses in Santa Ana, the idea was being molded by us, and somehow, with neither practice nor
studies in the production of alcohol beverages, we went specializing in the creation of these.

Before being able to deal with different types of fruits, and their own sugar levels, and acidity,
our project was judged 3 times; which helped us to change many things and improve a lot. One thing we
changed was the bar name which was intended to be called Piz En Lov, a name that many people thought it
had double meaning. In our third evaluation we were the happiest team because we were very well criticized,
giving us the guideline to follow. Since the judges expressed that our products have great taste. We
concluded that it was our idea, our dream, in addition to having the necessary skills, and so it was
in every class, we were grounding the ideas of our small business that we call Lov En Piz.

Currently we (Ever Guerra, Ronald Tejada and Stephannie Zúñiga) are glad of what we have made
as the Lov En Piz Bar creators and owners.
